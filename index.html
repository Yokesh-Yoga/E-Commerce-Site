About Us

Marudham Foods is a well-loved South Indian vegetarian restaurant located in the lively neighborhood of Porur, Chennai, renowned for its authentic, home-style meals served at highly affordable prices. Situated conveniently on Raju Street, Ramakrishna Nagar, Marudham Foods has carved out a loyal following among local residents, office-goers, students, and families, thanks to its unwavering commitment to traditional taste, cleanliness, and friendly service. The restaurant specializes in a range of classic South Indian dishes such as soft idlis, crispy dosas, flavorful pongal, hot vadas, and comforting upma, all accompanied by aromatic sambar, tangy rasam, and a variety of chutneys that showcase the region’s culinary richness. What makes Marudham Foods stand out is not just the quality of its dishes but the homely feel they offer—every meal is reminiscent of traditional Tamil kitchen cooking, simple yet deeply satisfying. Patrons often mention the freshness of ingredients and the consistency in flavor, making it a go-to spot for those seeking a reliable and familiar taste. The pricing is another major draw, with an average cost for two people being around ₹300, which is highly reasonable for the quality and quantity provided. Marudham Foods is especially popular during breakfast and lunch hours, but also sees a steady stream of diners during its dinner service. Operating in two shifts—8:00 AM to 3:30 PM and 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM—the restaurant caters to both early risers and late diners, ensuring flexibility for all types of customers. Unlike many trendy restaurants that focus on modern or fusion cuisine, Marudham Foods remains firmly rooted in tradition, which is precisely what its patrons value most. Some confusion exists with a similarly named brand, Marutham Foods, which sells organic groceries and millets online, but the two are unrelated; the Porur-based Marudham Foods is strictly a restaurant known for its hot, flavorful meals.
